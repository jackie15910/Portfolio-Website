About Me

Born in Toronto Canada, I grew up in the GTA, moving from Milton to Oakville and now Mississauga, I've helped with the salon family business before aspiring to be a great full stack developer and programmer.

My coding journey began in high school with a semester course. Young and naive, I was distracted by online games and friends, and didn't take it seriously, doing poorly, I withdrew from the course. Years later, after exploring various jobs and becoming a hairdresser, I sought a more fulfilling career. Inspired by my younger brother and friends, successful software developers, I decided to give coding another try. Initially skeptical, I started learning on my own through videos and projects. The freedom, variety, critical thinking, and problem-solving aspects of coding captivated me and I was hooked. Determined, I enrolled in a web development bootcamp, I studied hard, and with the help of my friends and brother, I graduated. After having completed a short-term front-end web development job, I am now looking for new opportunities.
